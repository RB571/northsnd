Northsnd revisits his formative years in music with Schoolwork, a five-part retrospective spanning 100 tracks produced between 1996 and 2010. Moving from happy hardcore and dreamhouse to trance and techno, the project captures both his personal and musical evolution during the pre-internet era. Now fully remastered with modern tools, including A.I., Schoolwork breathes new life into his teenage creations and reframes them for today’s listeners. The project comprises five albums (100 tracks in total), alongside two compilation albums, one remix album, one collaboration album, eight EPs, and sixty singles, making Schoolwork one of the most extensive archival releases in electronic music. Schoolwork Vol. 1 (Experiment) and Vol. 2 (Experience) were mostly completed between 1996 and 1999, while Vol. 3 – Vol. 5 feature rediscovered and newly finished tracks that bridge Northsnd’s past and present sound.
