Northsnd revisits his formative years in music with ‘Schoolwork’, a five-part retrospective spanning 100 tracks produced between 1996 and 2014. Moving from happy hardcore and dreamhouse to trance and techno, the project captures both his personal and musical growth during the pre-internet era. Now fully remastered with modern tools, including A.I., ‘Schoolwork’ breathes new life into his teenage creations and frames them for today’s listeners.

The project comprises 5 albums (100 tracks in total), alongside 2 compilation albums, 1 remix album, 8 EPs, and 60 singles, making Schoolwork one of the most extensive archival releases in electronic music.
